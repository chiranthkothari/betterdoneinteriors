Our story

Driven by purpose.
Designed for comfort.

Better Done Interiors began with a simple observation: too many homeowners were getting burned by interior projects that ran over budget, behind schedule, and heavy on stress.

FounderSanjeev Telkar
A different kind of studio

Not louder or flashier—just more reliable.

We set out to be different. Not louder or flashier—just more transparent and more genuinely invested in each client’s experience.

Today, we’re the team Bangalore homeowners turn to when they want a partner they can trust completely. Someone who will tell them the truth about timelines, be upfront about costs, use quality materials, and actually show up when they say they will.

What drives us

The standards behind the work.

01

Your home, your story

Every family has a unique rhythm—how you cook, where you work, how you unwind. We design around that reality, not around a catalogue.

02

Transparency as standard

You’ll always know what’s happening, what it costs, and why. Milestone-based payments, detailed documentation, and honest conversations throughout.

03

Quality that lasts

We source materials from trusted manufacturers, conduct quality checks at every stage, and stand behind our work with post-project support.

04

Timelines you can trust

Realistic planning, dedicated project management, and consistent communication mean we deliver when we say we will.

05

Relationships outlast projects

We’re here after handover—for maintenance, upgrades, or just advice. Because we’re building partnerships, not transactions.
