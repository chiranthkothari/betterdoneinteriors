What we do

Every stage of your home journey.

Comprehensive interior services whether you’re buying, renovating, or simply maintaining your space—delivered by one trusted team.

01

Before Purchase & Possession

Professional property assessment before you buy or take possession—spotting layout issues, defects, and Vastu concerns that could cost you later.

Know what you’re getting before you sign.
  • Layout inspection and inputs
  • Home inspection & snag list creation
  • Possession-day assistance
  • Interiors-ready & Vastu readiness assessment

Who this helps

Homebuyers who want professional eyes on a property before finalising purchase or taking possession. We identify issues, layout limitations, and Vastu considerations early—saving you from expensive surprises.

02

Plan Your Home & Vastu

Professional planning sessions that help you visualise possibilities, assess space potential, and make informed decisions before starting work.

Get clear on possibilities before you commit.
  • Space planning & layout optimisation
  • Design concept development
  • Kitchen & storage needs assessment
  • Budget & scope coaching
  • Vastu pre-purchase review & consultation for new homes

Who this helps

Anyone starting to think about interiors but unsure about design direction, feasibility, or costs. Expert clarity without requiring a full project commitment.

03
Our flagship service

Turnkey Home Interiors

End-to-end interior solutions covering design, execution, and coordination—delivering a fully finished, ready-to-live-in home.

Complete interiors, completely managed.
  • Full turnkey interiors (1, 2, 3 BHK, villas)
  • Modular-only turnkey (kitchen + wardrobes)
  • Deep renovation + turnkey package

Who this helps

Homeowners who want complete execution by a single trusted team. We manage design, procurement, civil work, carpentry, electrical, plumbing, and styling—delivering a move-in-ready home with transparent timelines and milestone-based payments.

04

During Work — Site Support

Independent quality monitoring and owner representation for NRIs and busy professionals who can’t be on-site regularly during their project.

Your trusted eyes on site.
  • Quality audits & owner’s representative services
  • Material & store selection assistance
  • NRI / remote owner monitoring

Who this helps

Clients who need professional oversight but can’t be on site regularly. Especially valuable for NRIs, busy professionals, or anyone wanting independent quality verification during execution.

05

After Interiors & Move-In

Post-completion detailing that transforms your finished construction into a polished, styled, ready-to-inhabit space.

Move into a finished home, not a worksite.
  • Post-interior deep cleaning
  • Final quality check & handover snagging
  • Styling & move-in setup
  • Move-in ready essentials coordination

Who this helps

Clients nearing completion who want a truly finished home—not just construction wrapped up. We handle final punch lists, thorough cleaning, and furniture arrangement.

06

Care Plans & Small Works

Ongoing maintenance programs and upgrade services to keep your interiors functioning beautifully long after project completion.

We’re here after the project ends.
  • Periodic health checks & tune-ups
  • Annual maintenance contracts for carpentry & cleaning
  • Upgrades & small works desk

Who this helps

Homeowners who want to keep their interiors in excellent condition long-term. Regular maintenance prevents small issues from becoming big problems.

07

Vastu for Existing Homes

Thoughtful Vastu analysis for occupied homes, suggesting achievable interior adjustments without requiring major reconstruction.

Thoughtful Vastu integration without upheaval.
  • Vastu analysis & detailed report
  • Vastu-focused interior upgrade recommendations

Who this helps

Homeowners who want to align their existing space with Vastu principles through practical modifications—realistic solutions that respect both your beliefs and your budget.
