01

Sudipta, Ian & Tina

Purva Zenium, Bangalore•3 BHK Apartment•Renovation

What they needed

Renovate their home interiors with changes including kitchen and wardrobe configurations, wall paneling, wallpaper and painting, shutters, wooden flooring, tiles, plumbing, soft furnishings and accessories.

The challenge

The renovation project was to be delivered whilst the family continued living at the project site.

Our solution

We segregated the whole house into three units—clean zone, work zone, and out-of-bounds zone. The clean zone was where the family lived. We sealed the out-of-bounds zone so it remained dust-free, and the work zone was cleaned every day.

Why it works for them

The clients now have a functional home. The kitchen and dining feel spacious despite holding more storage than the original design. The walls and floors have come alive, and the aesthetic they wanted is delivered—free from clutter.

Project highlights

Spacious dining Relaxing terrace Premium finish walls Custom flooring & doors High-end shower & washroom elements On time · transparent milestone payments
02

Vanishree & Shivaprasad

Yelahanka•2 BHK Apartment•Turnkey Interiors

The brief

A comfortable, flexible home for a young professional couple who wanted every space to feel thoughtful and personal—not template-driven.

What we were working with

A standard 2 BHK layout with poor light and limited storage. The couple had different style preferences and needed the design to reflect both their tastes without feeling disjointed.

The approach

We spent extra time in discovery understanding their routines and long-term plans. A neutral palette formed the foundation, layered with distinct accents, textures, and furniture per room. Storage was built into every possible space, and magnetic track lights eliminated the need for a false ceiling while improving the aesthetics.

The result

They describe the journey as comfortable and collaborative. The home feels bright and unified despite reflecting two different personalities—and they genuinely enjoy spending time in every room, which was the whole point.

What might interest you

Solutions where natural light is sparse Multi-functional furniture A process that listens to both partners Mix-and-match cohesion without uniformity Completed in 45 days
